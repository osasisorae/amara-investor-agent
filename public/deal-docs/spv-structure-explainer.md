# SPV Structure Explainer

## Why an SPV is used

The Akwa Ibom opportunity is structured through a special purpose vehicle so investor capital, project assets, and project-level reporting remain ring-fenced from FutureX operating activities.

## What investors own

Investors subscribe for a fractional economic interest in the SPV. The SPV holds legal ownership of the project assets and receives the project revenues before expenses, reserves, and distributions are processed.

## Why this matters

- project cashflows are tracked at the vehicle level
- reporting can be produced against one dedicated asset structure
- investor rights are documented in the subscription and operating agreements
- payment instructions can be tied to one lead-specific reference per investor

## What to ask in diligence

- what reporting cadence will be provided
- how distributions are approved
- what happens at exit
- how investor votes or consents are handled for material decisions
