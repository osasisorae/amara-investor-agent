# Akwa Ibom Hospitality SPV — Deal Brief

## Snapshot

- Vehicle: Akwa Ibom Hospitality SPV
- Minimum ticket: ₦5,000,000
- Hold period: 5 years
- Target return framing: 18% annual appreciation target
- Exit path: Sale or refinance at the end of the hold period

## What the vehicle owns

The SPV funds and owns the Akwa Ibom hospitality project, including the land, hotel buildout, lounge operations, and supporting infrastructure. Investors subscribe into the SPV rather than buying an isolated room or unit.

## Revenue logic

The operating model blends room revenue, food and beverage revenue, and lounge contribution. FutureX manages the project and distributes investor proceeds according to the governing deal documents.

## What to review next

Use the deal room conversation to ask about:

- target returns and downside cases
- SPV governance and reporting
- FX and repatriation considerations
- construction and operating risks
- KYC and onboarding timing
